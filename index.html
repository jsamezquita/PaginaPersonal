Academic formation

When I was little, I studied in Gimnasio el Lago where I completed the first year of my elementary studies. It was a really big school, I did not have many friends but I used to go clay after classes, which were very entertaining. When I took the school bus with my brother I used to have long conversations with his friends who where very kind to me.

After finishing my first year in elementary studies, I was matriculated in Colegio Militar Simón Bolivar where I studied until I graduated. I was and outstanding student for the first and last 3 years while I attented classes in this school. Because it was a Military School, there was no science, humanity or arts branches to "specialize". Instead, we were given military instruction on fridays and saturdays. During this instruction days I learned a lot about the military lifestyle.

I got matriculated in software engineering at Universidad de los Andes in the 2020-02 term. I am currently coursing my 7th semester and I am looking forward to graduate next semester. I really like spending time inside the university campus so I used to stay to 9:00 p.m, most of the time working in Waira ML612/613.
